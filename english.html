Dr. Khoramshahi is working in the clinics located in Pasdaran Avenue and Madani Street, Tehran, Iran. Both centers are equipped with the most advanced sterilization systems and all modern dental services are offered using modern devices, knowledge and experience of the dentist. Our main goal is to maintain your health and provide the highest quality of care

Services

At this center, examinations, counseling and treatment plans for all of the following services are free of charge by appointment

Restorative

In the past, the concept of restorative was just filling the tooth, but today it means restoring and restoring the structure and natural appearance of the tooth. To this end, it is necessary to have new, high quality and tooth-colored materials and, on the other hand, to attend additional beauty courses

Implant

The best replacement for missing teeth is implant. Dr. Khoramshahi has been active in this field since participating in valid courses and having multiple degrees since 2007, and with the introduction of thousands of implants with great expertise in this field

Laminate

Laminate is the most beautiful dentistry treatment that is made using advanced technology of thin sheet of porcelain and adheres to the tooth surface to create a beautiful smile

Venier Composite

An affordable way to create a Hollywood smile and the creation of natural teeth that is heavily dependent on art, experience and quality materials. See the section of the album to see the portfolio

Perio

A discipline of dentistry that deals with gum care and treatment. This section focuses on the prevention of gum disease. All patients undergo oral and dental health education and, if necessary, periodic examinations and timely scaling of gum disease are prevented. In the event of a disease, advanced gum surgery can be done at the center

Endo

Nasal irradiation and root canal treatment are essential when caries get to the brain and root of the tooth. All root treatments are provided at the center with advanced devices and new technology

Orthodontic

The orthodontic in this center is done in two moving and fixed ways. The best treatment age is after 12 years of age, but sometimes some measures are required at the age of 7 years. Having regular teeth at any age is possible that we provide for you

Prosthesis

In this section, using German and Swiss bursaries and LED turbines, the subcutaneous shiny finishes are sharpened with minimal damage to the teeth, and the Feldspathic, E-Max and Zirconia beadings are made. Cooperates with the most reputable Iranian labs to provide coatings
